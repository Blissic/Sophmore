Cases and Form Factors

PC cases come in various form factors to suit different needs, ranging from compact **Mini-ITX** builds for portability to spacious **Full-Tower** cases for high-performance systems. **Micro-ATX (mATX)** offers a balance of size and expandability, while standard **ATX** mid-towers are popular for gaming and general use. **Extended ATX (E-ATX)** supports high-end motherboards and multi-GPU setups, and **Full-Tower** cases provide maximum cooling and storage for extreme builds.
