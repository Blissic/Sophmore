UEFI/BIOS

The BIOS is your PC's startup boss. It checks your hardware, wakes up parts like the CPU and RAM, and gets your operating system running. It's also where you can change important settings before your computer fully starts. Think of it like a computer's "setup menu" that makes sure everything works together from the moment you press power.
