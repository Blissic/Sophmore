Motherboards

The motherboard is the backbone of a PC, connecting all critical components—CPU, RAM, GPU, storage, and peripherals—so they can communicate. It provides power, data pathways, and expansion slots, ensuring everything works together seamlessly. Think of it as the nervous system of your computer!
