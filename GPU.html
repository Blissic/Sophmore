Video Card

The video card (or GPU) is what makes everything appear on your screen. It takes data from your computer and turns it into pictures, games, and videos. A good GPU makes games look smooth and pretty, helps with video editing, and can even speed up some tasks. The more powerful your video card, the better it can handle fancy graphics and high resolutions.
